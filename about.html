FAQ

Questions I get asked a lot, especially at job interviews


Can you do a case study for us?
Absolutely not. Next question!
How do I know you're the right designer for us?
My credentials speak for themselves. Let's have a chat and see how it feels.
Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
Same place I am today; alive and well, energetic and happy, with my family and always crafting.
What's your T-shape?
I'm a visual person and my strong suit is visuals, UI design and interaction design. I enjoy creating clean and minimalist graphics and working with visuals to communicate. I also feel highly confident in understanding human motivations, wireframing concepts and testing ideas through prototypes.
What's a strong skill you have?
Empathy, adapting to change and creativity.
What do you suck at?
Keeping in touch with friends, daily workout, reading books, keeping up a good diet.
What are you good at?
Human interactions, design, aesthetics, presentations, tech, sports, communication, working, conversations, legos, DIY.
How do you deal with imposter syndrome?
I'm aware it's there! I'm trying to make the best of it. I'm a perfectionist, working with utmost precision, making sure my work caters to expectations but I also prefer progress over perfection. Also, knowing that every maker is going through the same feeling is a relief.
How do you motivate yourself?
Future, Music, Family, Food, Outdoors, Design, Comedy.
How do you find inspiration?
Album Covers, Space, History, Isolation, Nature.
Any Hobbies
I love food, outdoors, coffee & chocolate. I love hanging out with friends. I love to travel and visit foreign destinations.
